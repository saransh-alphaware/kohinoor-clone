Recognized Excellence in Curtain Accessories Manufacturing Across India

As a leading curtain accessories and track manufacturer in India, Kohinoor has earned recognition from industry bodies, trade associations, and retail partners across the nation. Our commitment to innovation, durability, and elegant design has made us a preferred choice for architects, interior designers, wholesalers, and retailers throughout Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, and beyond.

We take pride in being not just a supplier, but a partner in creating beautiful, functional window treatments that transform living spaces across India.

Frequently Asked Questions About Our Awards

What makes Kohinoor an award-winning curtain accessories manufacturer?

Kohinoor has earned multiple awards for our commitment to quality, innovation in design, use of premium imported materials, sustainable manufacturing practices, and exceptional customer service. Our 25+ years of experience and ISO certifications validate our position as an industry leader in India.

Which organizations have recognized Kohinoor?

We have been recognized by prestigious organizations including the Interior Design Excellence Awards, National Manufacturing Summit, Indian Home Decor Association, Retail Excellence Awards, Green India Initiative, and India Trade Excellence among others.

Are Kohinoor products certified for quality?

Yes, our manufacturing processes are ISO 9001:2015 certified, and selected product ranges carry BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) certification. We maintain strict quality control throughout our production process to ensure every curtain accessory meets international standards.

What product categories have won awards?

Our award-winning product range includes aluminium curtain tracks, designer finials, luxury tassels, steel pipes and rods, curtain accessories (hooks, rings, brackets), and elegant tie backs. Each category reflects our commitment to quality and design excellence.

How can I purchase Kohinoor's award-winning products?

Our products are available through authorized retailers and wholesalers across India, as well as through our direct sales channel. For bulk orders, wholesale inquiries, or to find a retailer near you, please contact us at +91 98213 39373 or visit our showroom in Mumbai.
