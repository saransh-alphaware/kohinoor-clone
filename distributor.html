Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment required?

The minimum investment typically ranges from ₹5-10 lakhs depending on the territory size and market potential. This covers initial inventory, display materials, and working capital.

How long does the application process take?

We typically review applications within 3 business days. The complete onboarding process, including verification and training, usually takes 2-3 weeks.

Do you provide exclusive territory rights?

Yes, we provide exclusive territory rights to qualified distributors based on their investment capacity and market coverage plan. This ensures healthy business growth without internal competition.

What kind of training and support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive product training, sales techniques workshops, marketing materials, product catalogs, display samples, and ongoing support from a dedicated relationship manager.

What are the payment terms?

Payment terms are discussed during the onboarding process and are typically based on your business profile and order volume. We offer flexible credit terms to established partners.

Can I distribute other brands alongside Kohinoor?

While we prefer dedicated focus on Kohinoor products, you can carry complementary product lines that don't directly compete with our curtain accessories range. This is discussed case-by-case.
